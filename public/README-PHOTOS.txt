# Profile Photo

Add your professional profile photo here.

## Required File:
profile.jpg

## Instructions:
1. Choose a professional headshot photo
2. Rename it to: profile.jpg (or profile.png)
3. Copy it to the /public/ folder (same level as this README)

## Recommendations:
- Size: 400x400px minimum (square format works best)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Professional appearance (business attire recommended)
- Good lighting and clear background
- High quality image

## Photo Tips:
✅ Smile and look approachable
✅ Professional attire (suit or formal wear)
✅ Neutral or simple background
✅ Face clearly visible
✅ Good resolution (not blurry)

Note: This photo appears in the Hero section as a circular profile image.
